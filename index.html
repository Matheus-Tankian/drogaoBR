About Brazilian
Hot Dog


The hot dog is an American-managed invention that combines bread with a delicious sausage, as well as ingredients such as sweet and sour sauce, cucumber-based pickles, mustard and infamous ketchup. Among Brazilians it is a little different, because the fillings vary a lot depending on the region of the country. Want to know how different places boost your snacks? Come along with us!
